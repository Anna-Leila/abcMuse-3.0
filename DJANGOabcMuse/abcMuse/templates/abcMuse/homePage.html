Welcome to demo version of mini-game abcMuse!

Rules:

You should choose the total number of vowels and consonants. The program will show the amount of every individual letter. Each new configuration will be random. You have to think of as many words with available letters as you can. Each letter with positive amount will raise your score by 1. If You use a letter with zero amount, your score will reduce by 1. Try to get the highest score possible.


If You enter several words at a time and your score is positive, the program will search for these words in “Hamlet” play by William Shakespeare. It will show you the line with maximum number of words in it, if this number is more than one.

This funny word game will cheer You up and become a mental gymnastics. Don’t forget to tell your friends about it! Good luck!
